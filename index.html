Our Story

A Family Built on
Trust & Craftsmanship

Empire Fine Jewellers is a family-owned luxury jewellery and watch boutique, offering bespoke creations and a curated selection of pre-owned Patek Philippe, Rolex, and Audemars Piguet timepieces. Our clients enjoy a personalised and exclusive buying experience, with access to GIA-certified loose diamonds and expert custom design, repair, and valuation services. Many of our clients return as lifelong friends, trusting us with their most treasured jewellery and timepieces.

Above & Beyond Quality

Elevate your style with jewellery that lasts a lifetime. We never compromise on quality. From expertly crafted wedding rings to unique custom pieces, every design is made to stand out, endure, and impress.

Sparkling Relationships

When you order from us, whether it's your first or tenth time, you become part of our family. During your search for the perfect gemstone or setting, we help you through every step of the process.

Our Promise

We are committed to honesty, integrity, and exceptional craftsmanship. We take pride in every piece we create, and we want you to feel that same pride every time you wear it.

Accreditation

National Association
of Jewellers

We are proud to be members of the National Association of Jewellers. Every NAJ member is required to abide by the NAJ's Code of Conduct based on honesty, integrity and professionalism. The grant of arms gives customers complete peace of mind that they are dealing with a jewellery business they can trust.
